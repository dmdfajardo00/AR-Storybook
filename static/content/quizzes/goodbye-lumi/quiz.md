- [ ] When you grow plants in a greenhouse
  > Nice try! While the name comes from greenhouses, the greenhouse effect is about gases trapping heat in Earth's atmosphere.

## Question 5

What happens when polar ice melts?

- [ ] Nothing changes
  > Nice try! Melting ice causes sea levels to rise and reduces Earth's ability to reflect sunlight, making warming worse.

- [ ] It creates more drinking water
  > Nice try! Melting polar ice actually raises sea levels and threatens coastal communities and polar animal habitats.

- [x] Sea levels rise and coastal areas can flood
  > That's right! Rising sea levels threaten coastal cities and change habitats for animals like polar bears and penguins.

- [ ] The ice grows back immediately
  > Nice try! Polar ice is melting faster than it can reform, causing sea levels to rise around the world.
