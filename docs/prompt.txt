STYLE NOTES FOR ALL PROMPTS
================================================================================

General Style Guidelines:
- Modern, clean vector illustration style
- Child-friendly but not childish (ages 8-14)
- Rounded corners and organic shapes throughout
- Soft gradients rather than flat colors
- Subtle shadows for depth (not harsh drop shadows)
- No text or letters in the icon
- Must work on both light and dark backgrounds
- Should be recognizable at small sizes (favicon)

Color Application:
- Primary: Canopy Green #22A652 (main element)
- Secondary: Ocean Deep #1E6B8C (accents, water elements)
- Accent: Sunset Coral #FF6B4A (highlights, glows)
- Background: Transparent or very light mint #F0F7F4

Avoid:
- Sharp corners or harsh geometric shapes
- Dark, scary, or apocalyptic imagery
- Overly complex details that won't scale down
- Generic "eco" cliches (no recycling symbols)
- Realistic photography style
- Text or wordmarks

Inspiration References:
- Duolingo app icon (friendly, character-driven)
- Headspace app icon (clean, rounded, calming)
- Modern children's book illustrations
- Studio Ghibli nature aesthetics
- Apple's SF Symbols design language


================================================================================
TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Output Specifications:
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Recommended generation size: 1024x1024 pixels
- File format: PNG with transparency
- Color space: sRGB

Required Sizes for Final Icons:
- 512x512 (App Store)
- 192x192 (Android)
- 180x180 (Apple Touch)
- 144x144 (PWA)
- 96x96
- 72x72
- 64x64
- 48x48
- 32x32 (Favicon)
- 16x16 (Favicon)

Note: Generate at highest quality, then resize down. The icon should
maintain its recognizability and appeal at all sizes.
